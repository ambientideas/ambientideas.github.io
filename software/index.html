Ambient Ideas software engineering division brings more than 15 years of enterprise software development experience to each project we collaborate on. We offer a complete range of contributions to projects, from Open Source Software (OSS) usage consulting through architecture verification up to complete custom application development.

Consulting

Ambient Ideas can bring the talents of successful enterprise application architecture and imple- mentation to your project to assist your existing development team or build an application from the ground up. When your firm has an existing development team in place that needs to be augmented, we especially pride ourselves in increasing your team's knowledge and capabilities during the development collaboration effort.

Additionally, our lead architect, Matthew McCullough, is a successful practitioner of bringing architecture to CEO level executives. He can adeptly gain corporate support for development decisions and budgets, as well as offering status presentations that both engage and explain in the perfect balance of technical and business language. Your next software project with Ambient Ideas' contributions will be the best software project you company has ever experienced.

Open Source

Ambient Ideas contributes to the ecosphere of Open Source events in Denver, Colorado metro area by speaking at such events at the Denver Java Users Group, Boulder Java Users Group, and Denver Open Source Users Group. Additionally, Ambient Ideas has held a position on the steering committee for the Denver Open Source Users Group since its inception.
